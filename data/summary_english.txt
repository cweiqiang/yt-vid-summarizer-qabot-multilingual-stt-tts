The Genshin Impact Version 3.8 Special Program has been released, and it
showcases all the new content coming to the game in the next update. The program
begins with a brief introduction to the new version, including the new event
Wishes, Hangout Events, and outfits. The program then transitions to a gameplay
segment where the hosts explore the new event area, Bottleland. They discuss the
new event mechanics, including the Preprints, Choo-Choo Cart, and Spino Blaster.
The program then ends with a preview of the new HoYo FEST events and the Genshin
Impact 3rd Commemorative OST album
