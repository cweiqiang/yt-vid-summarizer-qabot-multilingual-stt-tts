Genshin Impact Version 3.8 Special Program  The Special Program is hosted by
Zach Aguilar, the voice of Aether. He is joined by Poonam Basu, the voice of
Klee, Risa Mei, the voice of Sangonomiya Kokomi, and Suzie Yeung, the voice of
