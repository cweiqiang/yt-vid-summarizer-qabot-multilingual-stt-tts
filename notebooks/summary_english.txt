The Genshin Impact Version 3.8 Special Program was a livestream event that aired
on June 29, 2023. The program revealed new information about the upcoming
version, including new characters, events, and areas.  The new characters that
were revealed are Klee and Eula, who will be returning with their own event
wishes. Kaeya will also be getting his own Hangout Event in Version 3.8.  The
new events that were revealed are the Secret Summer Paradise event, which is a
gift from Alice to her daughter Klee, and the Perilous Expedition event, which
is a combat event
